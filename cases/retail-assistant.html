E-commerce AI Assistant Increases Conversions by 18%

How we built an intelligent shopping assistant that transformed customer experience and drove significant revenue growth.

Industry E-commerce
Timeline 8 weeks
Team Size 3 specialists

The Challenge

RetailMax, a mid-size e-commerce company, was struggling with high cart abandonment rates (68%) and overwhelming customer support volume. Their existing help desk couldn't keep up with product questions, sizing inquiries, and general shopping assistance.

Key pain points included:

  • Long response times for customer inquiries (avg. 4-6 hours)
  • Inconsistent product recommendations
  • High cart abandonment during decision-making
  • Support team overwhelmed with routine questions

Our Approach

We designed and implemented a comprehensive AI assistant that combines natural language processing with their existing product catalog and customer data.

Technical Stack

OpenAI GPT-4 React.js Node.js MongoDB Stripe API Shopify API

Key Features Implemented

  • Intelligent Product Recommendations: AI analyzes browsing behavior and preferences to suggest relevant products
  • Real-time Chat Support: Instant answers to sizing, shipping, and product questions
  • Abandoned Cart Recovery: Proactive outreach when customers hesitate at checkout
  • Visual Search: Customers can upload images to find similar products

Results & Impact

+18% Conversion Rate

From 2.3% to 2.7% across all traffic sources

−34% Support Tickets

AI handles 80% of routine inquiries automatically

$2.3M Revenue Impact

Additional revenue in first 6 months

−68% Cart Abandonment

Proactive assistance reduces hesitation
